Meet Joe Taylor

Born and Raised

Joe Taylor was born and raised in Omaha, Nebraska. He grew up on a farm, where he loved to play outside with his brother and cousins. He has many memories of great times including enjoying homecooked meals and sledding with family. While in school, he loved to explore new hobbies and participate in many different school sports. As he got older, he wanted to become an Oil Company Executive. This idea stemmed from the show Dallas.

Jobs and Careers

For a starter job, Joe worked for a florist in the North Omaha area. While working there he helped with various landscaping techniques. He utilized these techniques and skills when he became a homeowner. The biggest lesson that Joe learned from this job experience was the value of hard work. He believes that every job will provide you with things to learn that can be used in the future.

Ultimately, Joe chose a career in technology. His interest in this field stemmed from taking an elective while in college that involved programming. He graduated with his Bachelor of Science in Business Administration in 1990. Since then, he became an Information Technology Engineer at Mutual of Omaha. He spent most of his working years with this company, helping them install, maintain, and develop their applications. His favorite part of the job was helping business users work better.

Interests and Fun

After 34 years with Mutual of Omaha, Joe decided to retire and spend some time enjoying the things that he loves. Though he is no longer working, he keeps himself busy golfing, attending sporting events, listening to live music shows, spending time with friends and family, and creating new projects of home modifications and maintenance.
